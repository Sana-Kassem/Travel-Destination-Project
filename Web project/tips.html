Best Travel Times for the top 10 most visited Arab countries(2024)

Discover the ideal months for visiting your destination, considering weather, events, and costs.

  • United Arab Emirates (Dubai, Abu Dhabi):

    Best time: Winter (November - March). Mild weather makes it ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

    • Average temperatures: 15-25°C in winter.
    • Top Activity: Visit the Burj Khalifa, Dubai Miracle Garden, and Louvre Abu Dhabi.
    • Event: Dubai Shopping Festival (Dec-Jan), and Abu Dhabi Grand Prix (Nov).
    • AffordabilityExpensive. The UAE is known for luxury hotels, fine dining, and high-end shopping. Budget travelers can still find affordable accommodations if booked in advance.
  • Egypt (Cairo, Luxor, Alexandria):

    Best time: Fall to spring (October - April). Comfortable weather for exploring ancient sites.

    • Average temperatures: 20-30°C in fall and spring.
    • Top Activity: Nile cruises, visits to the pyramids, and hot air balloon rides in Luxor.
    • Event: Abu Simbel Sun Festival in February and October.
    • Affordability: Affordable. Egypt offers budget-friendly travel options, including low-cost accommodation, affordable food, and reasonably priced transport options.
  • Morocco (Marrakech, Fes, Casablanca):

    Best time: Spring and fall (March-May, Sept-Nov). Enjoy pleasant weather for exploring souks and landmarks.

    • Average temperatures: 15-25°C in spring and fall.
    • Top Activity: Wander through Marrakech’s souks, hike in the Atlas Mountains, and visit coastal Essaouira.
    • Event: Marrakech International Film Festival in November, Gnaoua World Music Festival in Essaouira (June).
    • Affordability: Moderate. Morocco offers affordable accommodations, food, and transportation, but luxury options can be expensive in certain tourist areas like Marrakech.
  • Saudi Arabia (Riyadh, Jeddah, Al-Ula):

    Best time: Winter (November - February). Pleasant temperatures for desert and historical site exploration.

    • Average temperatures: 12-25°C in winter.
    • Top Activity: Visit Diriyah, explore Al-Ula's rock formations, and relax in Jeddah's Red Sea resorts.
    • Event: Winter at Tantora Festival in Al-Ula (Dec-Feb).
    • Affordability:Moderate to Expensive. While Saudi Arabia offers budget options, high-end hotels and luxury experiences, especially in Riyadh and Jeddah, can make it a more expensive destination.
  • Jordan (Amman, Petra, Wadi Rum):

    Best time: Spring and fall (March-May, Sept-Nov). Mild weather for exploring Petra and the desert.

    • Average temperatures:20-30°C in spring and fall.
    • Top Activity: Hike through Petra, stargaze in Wadi Rum, and float in the Dead Sea.
    • Event: Jerash Festival of Culture and Arts (July-August).
    • Affordability: Moderate. Jordan is relatively affordable, especially for budget travelers, though Petra and other tourist sites can be pricey.
  • Tunisia (Tunis, Sousse, Djerba):

    Best time: Spring (April - June) and fall (September - October) for pleasant beach and sightseeing weather.

    • Average temperatures: 20-25°C in spring and fall.
    • Top Activity: Explore Carthage, relax on Mediterranean beaches, and visit the Medina of Tunis.
    • Event: International Festival of Carthage (July-August).
    • Affordability: Affordable. Tunisia is a great destination for budget travelers, with low-cost accommodation, inexpensive public transportation, and affordable meals.
  • Qatar (Doha):

    Best time: Winter (November - March). Comfortable weather for outdoor activities and city exploration.

    • Average temperatures: 15-25°C in winter.
    • Top Activity: Visit the Museum of Islamic Art, the Corniche, and Souq Waqif.
    • Event: Doha Cultural Festival and Qatar National Day (December 18).
    • Affordability: Expensive. Qatar is known for luxury hotels, fine dining, and shopping, making it a more expensive destination.
  • Lebanon (Beirut, Byblos, Baalbek):

    Best time: Spring (April - June) and fall (September - November). Pleasant temperatures for sightseeing.

    • Average temperatures: 15-25°C in spring and fall.
    • Top Activity: Visit ancient ruins in Byblos, explore Beirut’s art scene, and hike in the Chouf Mountains.
    • Event: Baalbek International Festival (July).
    • Affordability: Moderate. Lebanon offers affordable accommodations and food, but luxury options and Beirut’s high-end attractions can make it pricey.
  • Oman (Muscat, Nizwa, Wahiba Sands):

    Best time: Winter (November - February). Perfect for exploring deserts, forts, and coastal areas.

    • Average temperatures: 15-25°C in winter.
    • Top Activity: Camp in Wahiba Sands, explore Muscat’s historic sites, and relax on pristine beaches.
    • Event: Muscat Festival (Jan-Feb).
    • Affordability: Moderate. Oman offers a mix of budget and luxury options, though some resorts and activities can be on the expensive side.
  • Bahrain (Manama):

    Best time: Winter (December - February). Mild temperatures make it great for city tours and outdoor activities.

    • Average temperatures:15-22°C in winter.
    • Top Activity: Visit the Bahrain Fort, Bahrain National Museum, and the traditional Manama Souq.
    • Event: Bahrain Grand Prix (March) and Bahrain International Music Festival (October).
    • Affordability: Moderate. Bahrain is a more affordable option compared to its Gulf neighbors, but it can still be expensive in certain tourist spots.

Best Travel Times for Top 10 Most Visited Non-Arab Countries

Explore the ideal seasons, activities, events, and cost expectations for some of the world's most popular destinations.

  • France (Paris, Nice, Lyon):

    Best time: Spring (April-June) and fall (September-October). Enjoy mild weather and fewer crowds.

    • Average temperatures:10-20°C in spring and fall.
    • Top Activity: Visit the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and the beaches of the French Riviera.
    • Event: Cannes Film Festival (May), Bastille Day (July 14).
    • Affordability: Moderate to Expensive. France has luxury options but also offers budget accommodations.
  • Spain (Barcelona, Madrid, Seville):

    Best time: Spring (March-May) and fall (September-November) for comfortable sightseeing weather.

    • Average temperatures: 15-25°C in spring and fall.
    • Top Activity: Explore Sagrada Familia, relax on the beaches, and enjoy flamenco shows.
    • Event: La Tomatina (August), Running of the Bulls (July).
    • Affordability: Moderate. Spain is generally affordable, with budget options widely available.
  • United States (New York, Los Angeles, Orlando):

    Best time: Varies by region. Generally, spring (April-May) and fall (September-October) for milder weather.

    • Average temperatures: Varies widely (10-30°C in spring/fall).
    • Top Activity: See Times Square, visit Disney World, and explore Hollywood.
    • Event: Thanksgiving (November), New York Fashion Week (September).
    • Affordability: Expensive. Major cities can be pricey, but budget travel is possible with planning.
  • Italy (Rome, Florence, Venice):

    Best time: Spring (April-June) and fall (September-October). Great for sightseeing and festivals.

    • Average temperatures: 15-25°C in spring and fall.
    • Top Activity: Visit the Colosseum, canals of Venice, and vineyards in Tuscany.
    • Event: Venice Carnival (February), Florence Biennale (October).
    • Affordability: Moderate. Italy has budget options, though Venice and Florence can be costly.
  • Thailand (Bangkok, Phuket, Chiang Mai):

    Best time: Winter (November-February) for cool, dry weather ideal for beach and temple visits.

    • Average temperatures: 20-30°C in winter.
    • Top Activity: Visit temples, enjoy Thai street food, and relax on tropical beaches.
    • Event: Songkran Festival (April), Loy Krathong (November).
    • Affordability: Affordable. Thailand is budget-friendly with affordable accommodations, food, and transport.
  • Japan (Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka):

    Best time: Spring (March-May) for cherry blossoms or fall (September-November) for foliage.

    • Average temperatures: 10-20°C in spring and fall.
    • Top Activity: See cherry blossoms, visit historic temples, and enjoy Tokyo's tech districts.
    • Event: Cherry Blossom Festival (March-April), Gion Matsuri in Kyoto (July).
    • Affordability: Moderate to Expensive. Japan can be costly, but budget travel options are available.
  • China (Beijing, Shanghai, Xi’an):

    Best time: Spring (April-May) and fall (September-October) for pleasant weather.

    • Average temperatures: 10-20°C in spring and fall.
    • Top Activity: Visit the Great Wall, the Forbidden City, and explore ancient Xi’an.
    • Event: Chinese New Year (January/February), Mid-Autumn Festival (September).
    • Affordability: Moderate. China offers a range of options, with affordable accommodations and local transport.
  • Turkey (Istanbul, Cappadocia, Antalya):

    Best time: Spring (April-May) and fall (September-October) for cooler temperatures.

    • Average temperatures: 15-25°C in spring and fall.
    • Top Activity: Explore Hagia Sophia, hot air ballooning in Cappadocia, and relax on Antalya beaches.
    • Event: Istanbul Tulip Festival (April), Cappadocia Balloon Festival (October).
    • Affordability: Moderate. Turkey offers great value, with affordable options for accommodations and food.
  • United Kingdom (London, Edinburgh, Manchester):

    Best time: Late spring (May-June) and fall (September) to avoid peak summer crowds.

    • Average temperatures: 10-20°C in spring and fall.
    • Top Activity: See Big Ben, Edinburgh Castle, and explore historical sites.
    • Event: Notting Hill Carnival (August), Edinburgh Festival Fringe (August).
    • Affordability: Expensive. The UK can be costly, especially in London, though budget options are available.
  • Germany (Berlin, Munich, Frankfurt):

    Best time: Late spring (May-June) and fall (September-October) for ideal sightseeing conditions.

    • Average temperatures: 10-20°C in spring and fall.
    • Top Activity: Explore Berlin Wall, attend Oktoberfest in Munich, and visit castles.
    • Event: Oktoberfest (September-October), Berlin International Film Festival (February).
    • Affordability: Moderate. Germany offers a range of options, with affordable hostels and transit passes available.
