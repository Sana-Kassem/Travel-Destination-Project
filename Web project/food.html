Palestin-Maqluba

Is a flavorful layered dish of rice, meat and vegetables such as eggplant, cauliflower, and potatoes.

Lebanon – Kibbeh

Ground lamb or beef mixed with bulgur and spices, shaped into balls or patties and fried or baked. It’s a popular Middle Eastern appetizer or main dish.

Mexico – Tacos

Soft or hard corn tortillas filled with a variety of meats, beans, cheese, and fresh toppings like cilantro, onions, and salsa. Each offers unique flavors.

Italy – Pizza Margherita

A classic Italian pizza topped with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, basil, and olive oil. Simple yet flavorful, it represents the colors of the Italian flag

Japan – Sushi

Vinegared rice served with raw or cooked fish, vegetables, and sometimes seaweed. Sushi has various forms, including nigiri, maki, and sashimi.

France – Coq au Vin

A slow-cooked dish of chicken braised with wine, mushrooms, onions, and herbs. This dish is rich and aromatic, capturing French country-style cooking.

India – Butter Chicken

Creamy, spiced tomato-based curry with marinated chicken pieces. Often enjoyed with naan or rice, it’s mildly spicy and deeply flavorful.

Turkey – Kebab

Skewered, grilled meat,often served with rice,salad,and pita bread.Varieties include lamb,chicken,and beef.

Egypt – Koshari

A mix of rice, lentils, pasta, and chickpeas, topped with tomato sauce and fried onions. It’s hearty and full of textures.

Iraq – Masgouf

Grilled fish seasoned with spices and slow-cooked over a wood fire. It’s often served with rice and vegetables.

Switzerland – Fondue

Melted cheese served in a communal pot, where diners dip bread cubes into the warm, gooey mixture. A cozy winter favorite.

Jordan – Mansaf

Lamb cooked in a sauce of fermented dried yogurt, served over rice. It’s Jordan’s national dish and is popular at gatherings.

Morocco – Couscous

A traditional North African dish made with steamed semolina, often served with a stew of meat and vegetables. It’s aromatic and filling.

United States – Hamburger

An iconic American food,it consists of a grilled beef patty served in a bun with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and pickles. It’s simple yet universally loved.

United Kingdom – Fish and Chips

Deep-fried battered fish served with crispy chips (fries) and often a side of mushy peas. It’s a staple British comfort food.

Saudi Arabia – Al Kabsa

A spiced rice dish cooked with meat (usually chicken or lamb) and flavored with cinnamon, cardamom, and other spices.

Olive Harvest Festival

Celebrates the annual olive harvest, a key agricultural tradition in Palestine, with cultural performances and local food.

Baalbeck International Festival

prestigious festival featuring performances of classical music, theater, and dance, set in the ancient Roman ruins of Baalbeck.

Día de los Muertos

A vibrant celebration honoring deceased loved ones, with colorful altars, parades, and traditional foods like sugar skulls and pan de muerto.

Carnival of Venice

Famous for its elaborate masks and costumes, this carnival celebrates Italian culture with parades, theater performances, and masquerade balls.

Hanami (Cherry Blossom Festival)

People gather to appreciate the fleeting beauty of cherry blossoms, enjoying picnics under blooming sakura trees.

Cannes Film Festival

One of the world’s most famous film festivals, showcasing global cinema with stars and screenings in the French Riviera.

Diwali

The festival of lights, celebrated with fireworks, sweets, and the lighting of lamps to honor the victory of light over darkness.

Tulip Festival

Celebrates the bloom of tulips across the city, with colorful flower displays and events in parks, especially in April.

Cairo International Film Festival

One of the oldest film festivals in the Arab world, showcasing international films, and honoring filmmakers with awards

Nowruz

The Persian New Year festival, celebrating the arrival of spring with family gatherings, feasts, and traditional music.

Fête de l'Escalade (Geneva)

Celebrates the city's victory over the Duke of Savoy’s forces in 1602. The festival includes parades, historical reenactments, and the traditional "Marmite" chocolate pots filled with marzipan.

Jerash Festival of Culture and Arts

: Held in the ancient city of Jerash, this festival showcases Jordanian and international music, dance, and theatrical performances.

Moussem of Tan-Tan

A vibrant cultural festival that showcases Morocco’s nomadic traditions, featuring music, dances, and camel races.

Thanksgiving

A family-centered holiday with feasts, especially turkey, parades, and football games to give thanks for the harvest.

Notting Hill Carnival

Celebrates Caribbean culture with a massive street parade, vibrant costumes, music, and food in London’s Notting Hill district.

Janadriyah Festival

A cultural festival celebrating Saudi heritage with traditional music, folk dances, camel races, and local crafts.
